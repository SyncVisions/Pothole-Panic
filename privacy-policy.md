# Privacy Policy

**Game:** Pothole Panic  
**Developer:** Sync Visions  
**Last updated:** April 2026

---

## 1. Overview

Sync Visions ("we", "us", "our") operates Pothole Panic. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Pothole Panic is designed to collect as little personal data as possible.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

Pothole Panic collects and stores the following data **locally on your device**:

- **Nickname / Player name** — a display name you choose in the game settings
- **Game progress** — high scores, coins earned, unlocked items, equipped skins
- **Game settings** — music and sound preferences
- **Mission progress** — daily mission completion records
- **Leaderboard scores** — scores saved for the local in-game leaderboard

We do **not** collect:

- Real names, email addresses, or contact information
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Payment information
- Any data from children under 13

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

The data listed above is used solely to:

- Save your game progress between sessions
- Display your chosen name on the in-game leaderboard
- Remember your preferences (music, sound, vehicle color)

---

## 4. Data Storage

Your data is stored:

- **Locally** on your device via browser localStorage
- **On your CrazyGames account** (when playing on CrazyGames.com) via the CrazyGames Data Module, which syncs your progress across devices

We do not operate our own servers or databases. No data is transmitted to Sync Visions.

---

## 5. Third-Party Services

When playing on **CrazyGames.com**, the CrazyGames platform may collect data in accordance with their own privacy policy, including serving advertisements. Please refer to the [CrazyGames Privacy Policy](https://www.crazygames.com/privacy-policy) for details.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

Pothole Panic is intended for players aged 13 and older, in compliance with PEGI 12 guidelines. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

---

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** the data stored about you (viewable in-game)
- **Delete** your data by clearing your browser's localStorage or uninstalling the app
- **Opt out** of data collection — since all data is stored locally, you can clear it at any time
